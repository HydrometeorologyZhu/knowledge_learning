# Iran: Some Good Books to Know Iran

To understand Iran better, we need to know its history. Here are some high-quality books I've selected:

**<Iran: A Modern History>**, by Abbas Amanat

**<伊朗五百年>** (Five Hundred Years of Iran), Chinese version

**<An Introduction to Shi'i Islam: The History and Doctrines of Twelver Shi'ism>**: This book is about religion. Although many young Iranians are not religious now, understanding Shi'i Islam is still a useful way to comprehend modern Iran.

**<Reading Lolita in Tehran>**
