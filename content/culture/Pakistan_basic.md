# Pakistan: A Basic Introduction

**Pakistan**, officially the **Islamic Republic of Pakistan**, is a country located in South Asia. It is the world's fifth-most populous country, with a population exceeding 240 million people.

## Geography

Pakistan is a land of diverse landscapes, ranging from arid deserts to lush green valleys and some of the world's highest mountains.

- **Borders:** It is bordered by India to the east, Afghanistan to the west, Iran to the southwest, and China to the northeast.
- **Coastline:** To the south, it has a coastline along the **Arabian Sea**.
- **Key Features:** The famous **Indus River** flows through the country. The north is home to some of the world's tallest mountains, including **K2**, the second-highest peak on Earth.

## Major Cities

- **Islamabad:** The capital city, known for its modern design and greenery.
- **Karachi:** The largest city and the main financial and industrial hub, located on the coast.
- **Lahore:** The cultural capital, famous for its rich history, gardens, and delicious food.

## People and Language

- **Population:** A young and diverse nation with many ethnic groups, including Punjabis, Pashtuns, Sindhis, and Baloch.
- **Languages:** The national language is **Urdu**, which serves as a lingua franca. **English** is widely used in government, corporate business, and higher education. Many other regional languages are spoken, such as Punjabi, Pashto, and Sindhi.

## Culture

Pakistani culture is rich, deeply rooted in Islamic traditions, and influenced by its history.

- **Hospitality:** Guests are treated with immense respect and warmth.
- **Food:** Known for its flavorful and spicy cuisine, including dishes like *Biryani* (spiced rice with meat), *Nihari* (slow-cooked stew), and various kebabs.
- **Clothing:** Traditional attire like *Shalwar Kameez* is commonly worn, along with modern western clothing.

## Economy

Pakistan has a developing economy with key sectors including agriculture (wheat, cotton, rice), textiles, and sports goods manufacturing. It is also known for producing high-quality surgical instruments and is a major exporter of rice.

## Interesting Facts

- It is home to one of the world's oldest civilizations, the **Indus Valley Civilization**.
- The **Karakoram Highway**, which connects Pakistan and China, is one of the highest paved international roads in the world.
- It is the only Muslim-majority country to have successfully produced and tested nuclear weapons.
